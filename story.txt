One fateful time, December 13, a small meteor whistled through the Earth's atmosphere, burning a thin yellow streak through the air. The meteor dropped into a stream of the illustrious small town, Castenoga.

That was the day the crows dropped out of the sky.  The day that the buildings made of porous concrete-plaster were all dissipated to dust. The day that every human being on Earth awakened to a small, wondrous change-- supernatural powers.
